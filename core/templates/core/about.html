About 50/30/20 Budget

What is the 50/30/20 Rule?

The 50/30/20 rule is a simple budgeting method that helps you manage your money effectively. Here's how it works:

  • 50% for Needs: Essential expenses that you can't avoid
  • 30% for Wants: Non-essential items that improve your quality of life
  • 20% for Savings: Future security and financial goals
Why Use This Method?

This budgeting approach is:

  • Simple to understand and implement
  • Flexible enough to adapt to different income levels
  • Balanced between current needs and future goals
  • Effective for building long-term financial stability
